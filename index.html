    Code of Conduct

    • Entry Fee:

      • An entry fee of Rs. 79/- per student is applicable for participation.

    • Entry Requirements:

      • Only individual entries will be accepted for the competition.

    • Eligibility:

      • First-year students are eligible to participate in the competition using the C language.
      • Second and third-year students can participate by coding in Java.

    • Evaluation Process:

      • First-year participants will undergo separate evaluation sessions.
      • Second and third-year participants will be evaluated collectively.

    • Refreshments:

      • Participants will be provided with refreshments during the event.

    • Prizes and Recognition:

      • Winners stand a chance to win exciting cash prizes.
      • Every participant will receive a certificate of participation.
